How this works


PokeGAN is a generator and discriminator system where it trains itself to generate high quality pokemon cards to output. It takes in all of the pokemon cards as a png and tries to recreate something similar.


When you enter into the website it has already created the generated pokemon cards it needs when you ask the website for them. It should look relatively like a new pokemon card.
